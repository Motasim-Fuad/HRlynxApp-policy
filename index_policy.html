1. Information We Collect

When you register or use HRlynx, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: first name, last name, and email address.
  • User Content: prompts, messages, and files you submit to generate AI responses.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: in-app interactions, device/OS type, app version, and crash/diagnostic data.
  • Subscription & Payments: processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store; we do not store your payment card details.

Note: If we later enable product analytics, it will be limited to performance and feature improvement (no cross-app advertising). We will update this Policy if that changes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, secure, and improve HRlynx.
  • Manage accounts, subscriptions, and billing.
  • Personalize your app experience (e.g., saved interactions).
  • Communicate important service updates and policy changes.
  • Detect, prevent, and address security incidents, fraud, or misuse.
  • Maintain compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Subscription & Auto-Renewal Information

  • Paid subscriptions are billed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Your account may be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the then-current price.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your platform account settings.
  • If you cancel, you retain premium access until the end of your current period; no partial refunds.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information and we do not "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising under the CPRA.

We may disclose limited data to trusted service providers acting as processors under written agreements, solely to perform services for us, such as:

  • Cloud hosting & infrastructure (to operate and secure the app)
  • AI processing (to generate responses from your prompts)
  • Email & notifications (transactional communications)
  • Diagnostics & crash reporting (to maintain performance and reliability)
  • Analytics (if enabled) (to understand feature usage and improve the service)

Advertising & Affiliate Links: HRlynx may display affiliate links or ads within its news or content pages, including participation in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. As an Amazon Associate, HRlynx earns from qualifying purchases. When you click an affiliate link, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you. No personal data is shared with advertisers beyond standard referral information required to track the transaction, and all sponsored or affiliate content is disclosed in accordance with FTC guidelines.

Note: If a future marketplace for independent HR coaches is launched, you will see clear, in-flow disclosures and can choose what to share to facilitate a booking.
